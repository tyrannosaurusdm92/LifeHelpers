Shared life filter

Motivation can be borrowed from novelty, body-doubling, rewards, and tiny starts. The goal is an easier doorway into the task.

Tiny version
  • Make the task visible.
  • Make it smaller than seems reasonable.
  • Add a timer, song, or body double.
  • Reward starting, not just finishing.
Access notes
  • Use in-site rewards for partial effort.
  • Pair hard tasks with comfort and recovery.
  • Let one person body-double without becoming responsible for the whole task.
Us notes
  • William's needs and Jasper's capacity both count.
  • Ask before helping; ask before escalating; name the smallest next step.
  • Pause means pause, not abandonment. I am coming back. I care about us. I need a smaller next step.
  • What is needed, what capacity exists, what can be smaller, and what can wait?
