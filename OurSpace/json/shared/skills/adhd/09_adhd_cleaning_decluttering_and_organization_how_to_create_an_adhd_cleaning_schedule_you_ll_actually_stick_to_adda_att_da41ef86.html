Shared life filter

Cleaning is access care, not a worth test. Use scent-safe, seated, tiny-zone cleaning and stop before flare, resentment, or breathing risk.

Tiny version
  • Pick one surface or one item type.
  • Put supplies within reach.
  • Work for 30 seconds to 5 minutes.
  • Bag, basket, wipe, or relocate one thing.
  • Stop and record the next tiny step.
Access notes
  • Default to fragrance-free/MCAS-aware supplies.
  • No scrubbing marathons, bending marathons, or heavy lifting.
  • Use chair/bed-level sorting, grabbers, rolling bins, and caregiver capacity checks.
Us notes
  • William's needs and Jasper's capacity both count.
  • Ask before helping; ask before escalating; name the smallest next step.
  • Pause means pause, not abandonment. I am coming back. I care about us. I need a smaller next step.
  • What is needed, what capacity exists, what can be smaller, and what can wait?
